Meet the Developers

Tai Chen and DaQunda Magwood, two fledgling Software Engineers, teamed up to bring you Snapogram! Their website puts a retro spin on photo-sharing by displaying photos as polaroid pictures. By clicking on the "Add a Pic" link, anyone can contribute their favorite photos to the image gallery.

To learn more about DaQunda's other projects, please visit her Github page at SimplyyQunda.

You can also check out Tai's Github projects at Mindlikewater.
