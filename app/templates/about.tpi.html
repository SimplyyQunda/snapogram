Meet the Developers

Tai Chen and DaQunda Magwood are two fledgling Software Developers who teamed up to bring you Snapogram! Their website puts a retro spin on photo-sharing by displaying photos as Polaroid pictures. Click on the "Add a Pic" link to contribute your favorite photos to the gallery!

To learn more about DaQunda's other projects, visit her Github page at SimplyyQunda.

You can also check out Tai's Github projects at Mindlikewater.
